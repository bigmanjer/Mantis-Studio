# IP Protection Checklist (CODEX: IP-PROTECTION-001)

## Immediate (Pre-V1)
- Assert copyright in app
- Add Terms of Service
- Add Privacy Policy
- Add proprietary LICENSE file
- Add footer legal links
- Declare IP ownership clearly

## Short Term (Post-V1)
- Register domain + email
- File copyright registration (optional but strong)
- Prepare trademark filing for “MANTIS Studio”
- Create company entity (LLC / Corp)

## Long Term (Scaling)
- Trademark logo
- Contributor agreement (if others join)
- Open-core licensing strategy (if desired)

## Reality Check
This setup:
- Makes MANTIS clearly yours
- Protects you from legal nonsense
- Signals professionalism to users & partners
- Does not scare writers away
- Establishes the same legal baseline as real SaaS products
